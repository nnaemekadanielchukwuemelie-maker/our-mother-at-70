# Our Mother at 70

A cinematic single-page tribute website celebrating the **70th birthday of Victoria Offodimma Chukwuemelie** — May 24, 2026.

Built lovingly by her family with handwritten letters, real photographs, and 70 reasons why she is so deeply loved.

## Live site

Once GitHub Pages finishes its first build (1–2 minutes after this repo is created), the site will be live at:

**https://nnaemekadanielchukwuemelie-maker.github.io/our-mother-at-70/**

## What's inside

- A countdown to the birthday
- A cinematic film slideshow scored by Dolly Parton's *"Eagle When She Flies"* (streamed from the official YouTube upload)
- 70 reasons we love her
- Tributes from her children, grandchildren, siblings, and beloved son
- A photo gallery spanning seven decades
- A memory wall of handwritten notes
- A Ken-Burns scrolling timeline of her life

## Tech

Pure **HTML5 + CSS3 + Vanilla JavaScript** — no frameworks, no build step, no dependencies.
Just open `index.html` in any modern browser.

Music: the slideshow uses the official YouTube IFrame Player API to stream Dolly Parton's *Eagle When She Flies* directly from YouTube (no audio files are redistributed by this repo). A fully original Web Audio synth piano composition serves as a silent fallback if YouTube is unreachable.
